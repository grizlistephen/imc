Directions

The Centre is located at Sunshine on the Morisset Peninsula on the west side of Lake Macquarie, between Newcastle and Sydney.

By Car: Take the Morisset exit from the M1 Motorway. Driving time from Morisset to the Centre is about 10-15 minutes.

Proceed through the town of Morisset, cross over the railway line and take the right turn into Fishery Point Road.

At the Telstra tower and school, Fishery Point Road takes a left turn. Continue on Fishery Point Road through Bonnells Bay and Mirrabooka to Cessnock Road. There is a large green water tank at this intersection. Turn right. The white gates to the Centre are about 500 metres down Cessnock Road, on the right.

By Train: There is a railway station at Morisset, on the City Rail Service between Sydney and Newcastle. The train line is the Newcastle and Central Coast Line. Trains are approximately hourly, and take up to 2 hours from Central station, Sydney. From Newcastle it is about 1 hour. Check timetables at www.cityrail.com.au

Taxi service from Morisset telephone:

By Air: Sydney Airport is recommended. Connect with the train service to Morriset from Sydney Central station.

Flights into Williamtown (Newcastle) Airport are not recommended. The airport is about 1hr 45mins from the centre but there is no direct public transport from this airport to Morisset.
